Our Story

About Ganga Aarti Team

Born from devotion, rooted in tradition, carried by divine grace

Est. 2009

The Ganga Aarti Team was founded in 2009 by a group of devoted priests and cultural custodians who believed that the sacred ritual of Ganga Aarti should reach every home, every celebration, and every soul seeking divine blessings.

Originating from the holy ghats of Varanasi, our team carries generations of spiritual knowledge, Vedic training, and an unwavering devotion to Maa Ganga. We have had the honour of performing aarti across India — from grand temple inaugurations to intimate family gatherings.
